## 2. Information we collect

### a. Location information
The App uses your device's location to forecast sky conditions for where you are or for spots you
save.
- **Precise location** is used **only when you tap "Use my current location"** or when you grant the
  permission, to determine the latitude/longitude and light-pollution rating of a spot.
- You can also **search and save locations manually** without sharing your device location.
- We use location to compute forecasts. **We do not sell your location** or use it for advertising.
- You can revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings; manually saved spots will
  still work.

### b. Account information (if accounts are enabled)
If you create an account, we collect what's needed to sign you in — for example your **email
address** [and/or your **Apple ID** via Sign in with Apple]. [If you do not offer accounts, delete
this section.]

### c. Notification information
If you enable alerts, we register a **push notification token** for your device and store your
**alert preferences** (score threshold, on/off, quiet hours) so we can send you "GO" alerts when the
sky is clear. You can turn notifications off at any time in the App or your device Settings.

### d. Purchase information
Subscriptions and the one-time purchase are processed by **Apple App Store** / **Google Play** [and
managed via **RevenueCat**]. We receive your **subscription status** (free, trial, premium) to unlock
features. **We never receive or store your full payment-card details** — those are handled by the
app store.

### e. Usage and device information
We [and our service providers] may collect limited technical data such as **app version**, **device
type/OS**, **crash logs**, and **basic usage analytics** to keep the App working and improve it.
[Name your analytics/crash tools here, e.g. "We use [Expo / Sentry / PostHog] for this." If you
collect none, say "We do not use third-party analytics."]

### f. Weather and sky data
To produce forecasts, the App sends the **coordinates of your selected location** (not your identity)
to third-party weather/astronomy data providers [e.g. Open-Meteo, 7Timer!]. These providers receive
location coordinates to return forecast data.

## 3. How we use information
We use the information above to:
- Calculate and display sky-condition forecasts and verdicts for your locations;
- Send the push notifications you've enabled (GO alerts and the GO-window lookahead);
- Manage your subscription and unlock Premium features;
- Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the App;
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your data for third-party advertising.
